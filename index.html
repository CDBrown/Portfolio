Other Projects

Never Ending Mall Logo

This was a logo that I designed for a fake mall as part of a group project in one of my Web Design Classes at DeVry University. The concept was for the text to circle around in a 'neverending' fashion. However, I was still just starting out in Graphic Design at the time. While the concept was a good one, in hindsight, I can see that I did not fully take the font type I had chosen in mind and therefore made it hard to fully read. This was a project that I have since learned from as a designer and because of that have chosen to showcase it to show how I've grown and am still growing.
